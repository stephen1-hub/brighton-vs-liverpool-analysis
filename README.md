# 📊 Brighton vs Liverpool – Match Analysis

## 🧾 Overview
This analysis explores the Brighton vs Liverpool match using expected goals (xG), shot maps, and situational data.

Brighton generated **2.18 xG** compared to Liverpool’s **0.88**, highlighting a clear advantage in chance quality.

---

## 🔍 Key Insights

### ⚽ Brighton
- Strong reliance on **set-pieces**, especially corners  
- Created high-quality chances in central areas  
- Underperformed slightly in finishing (2 goals from 2.18 xG)

### ⚽ Liverpool
- Relied heavily on **transitions and fast breaks**  
- Struggled to create consistent high-quality chances  
- Lack of central penetration in the final third  

---

## 🗺️ Shot Map Insight
Brighton’s shots were concentrated in **high-danger central zones**, while Liverpool’s were more dispersed and lower quality.

---

## 🧠 Recommendations

### Brighton
- Maintain set-piece strength  
- Improve finishing efficiency  
- Add more variation in open play  

### Liverpool
- Increase central attacking presence  
- Improve structured build-up in the final third  
- Reduce reliance on transitions  

---

## 📌 Key Takeaway
**Shot quality > shot quantity**

Teams that consistently create high-probability chances gain a decisive edge.
